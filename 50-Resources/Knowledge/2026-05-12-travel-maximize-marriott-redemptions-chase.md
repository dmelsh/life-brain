# Maximize Marriott Redemptions with Chase Transfer Bonus

## Summary
Chase is offering a 65% transfer bonus to Marriott Bonvoy points until May 15th, allowing for significant savings on luxury hotel stays. By combining this bonus with Marriott's '5th night free' benefit, Chase cardholders can unlock exceptional value on high-end redemptions, such as the St. Regis Maldives, Mandapa Ritz-Carlton Reserve, and St. Regis Rome.

## Key Points
- Chase offers a 65% transfer bonus to Marriott points until May 15th.
- Combining the transfer bonus with Marriott's '5th night free' can lead to significant value.
- Example redemptions include St. Regis Maldives (3.1cpp value from Chase points), Mandapa Ritz-Carlton Reserve (1.9cpp value), and St. Regis Rome (2.6cpp value).
- These strategies allow users to book luxury hotels for a fraction of their cash price.
- It is advised not to transfer points speculatively; only transfer if a specific redemption is planned.

## Notable
> That is an incredible arbitrage opportunity from a Marriott redemption.
